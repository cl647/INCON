About the Team

The Intelligent Neuroimaging for Precision Neuroscience (INPN) group, led by Prof. Chao Li at the University of Cambridge, focuses on developing computational methods to decode the complexity of brain diseases. Our research integrates multi-modal MRI, tissue pathology, and spatial transcriptomics.

Principal Investigator

Prof. Chao Li

University of Cambridge, UK

Prof. Li's research focuses on the intersection of AI and Clinical Neuroscience. He has published extensively in top-tier journals including Nature Communications, NeuroImage, and MICCAI.
