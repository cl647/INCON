About the Team

The Intelligent Neuroimaging for Precision Neuroscience (INPN) group, led by Prof. Chao Li at the University of Cambridge, focuses on developing cutting-edge computational methods to decode the complexity of brain diseases. Our research integrates multi-modal MRI, tissue pathology, and spatial transcriptomics to drive the future of precision medicine.

Principal Investigator

Prof. Chao Li

Senior Research Fellow / Professor
Department of Clinical Neurosciences, University of Cambridge, UK

Prof. Li’s research focuses on the intersection of AI and Clinical Neuroscience. He has published extensively in top-tier journals including Nature Communications, NeuroImage, and MICCAI.
